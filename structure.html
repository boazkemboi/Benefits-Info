Benefits for Contributors

  1. Financial Growth & Returns
    • Earnings from Referrals:
      • KES 800 for each direct referral (Level 1).
      • KES 100 for each indirect referral (Level 2).
    • Interest on Contributions:
      • Short-term loans (≤30 days): 5% interest.
      • Medium-term loans (31–90 days): 10% interest.
      • Long-term loans (>90 days): 15% interest.
    • Annual/Periodic Profit Distribution: Members receive a share of accumulated profits.
  2. Loan Access & Financial Support
    • Eligibility: Loans available after one month of contributions.
    • Loan Amount: Up to 3× total contributions.
    • Flexible Repayment: Daily to monthly options.
    • Late Payment Penalty: KES 50 per missed installment.
  3. Security & Emergency Support
    • Guarantor System: Loans require two group guarantors for security.
    • Collateral Policy: 20% of the loan is held as collateral until full repayment.
    • Emergency Loan Provision:
      • Fast approval within 24 hours.
      • Maximum loan: 50% of total contributions.
      • Flat 3% processing fee, repayable within 60 days at 7% interest.
  4. Savings & Investment Opportunities
    • Regular Savings: Weekly contributions (e.g., KES 50) encourage financial discipline.
    • Investment Opportunities: Members can pool funds for freelancing, e-commerce, trade, and agriculture investments.
  5. Group Participation & Decision-Making
    • Regular Meetings: Monthly WhatsApp/in-person financial discussions.
    • Voting Rights: Members have voting rights in financial decisions and policy updates.
