💼 Benefits for Contributors

💰 Financial Growth & Returns

  • 💸 Earnings from Referrals
    • KES 800 for each direct referral (Level 1)
    • KES 100 for each indirect referral (Level 2)
  • 📈 Interest on Contributions (via Loan Profits)
    • Group earns interest through issued loans:
    • 🕐 Short-term loans (≤30 days): 5% interest
    • 🗓 Medium-term loans (31–90 days): 10% interest
    • 📅 Long-term loans (>90 days): 15% interest
    • 💵 80% of loan interest income is distributed to contributors as dividends, based on contribution size
    • 🏦 20% of loan interest is reserved for group sustainability, emergencies, and reinvestment
  • 📊 Annual/Quarterly Profit Distribution
    • Contributors receive dividends proportionally based on their contributions
    • A minimum of KES 40 in dividends is required for payout
    • Dividends below KES 40 are rolled over to the next period

📘 Dividend Calculation Example

  • Step 1: The group earns KES 1,000 from loan interest.
  • Step 2: Hold back 20% for reserve:
    • Reserve = 20% × 1,000 = KES 200
    • Dividends Available = 1,000 − 200 = KES 800
  • Step 3: Total group contributions = KES 10,000
  • Step 4: Dividend Rate = 800 ÷ 10,000 = 0.08 (8%)
  • Step 5: Apply to each member:
    • Member A (KES 200): 200 × 0.08 = 16 ❌ (Below KES 40, not paid)
    • Member B (KES 500): 500 × 0.08 = KES 40
    • Member C (KES 1,000): 1,000 × 0.08 = KES 80
    • Member D (KES 2,000): 2,000 × 0.08 = KES 160

💡 Dividend Policy for Members with Outstanding Loans

  • 🔁 Members with outstanding loans still earn dividends based on their contributions.
  • 💳 However, their dividends are automatically applied to reduce their outstanding loan balance.
  • 📉 This helps them repay loans faster and ensures group funds are protected.
  • 📢 Members are notified when their dividends are used for loan repayment.
  • ❗ Members who fully clear their loans will receive cash dividends in the next cycle.

🧾 Loan Access & Financial Support

  • ✅ Eligibility: Access loans after just 1 month of consistent contributions
  • 💳 Loan Amount: Up to 3× total contributions
  • 📆 Flexible Repayment: Daily, weekly, or monthly options
  • ⚠️ Late Payment Penalty: KES 50 per missed installment

🛡 Security & Emergency Support

  • 👥 Guarantor System: Requires 2 guarantors from the group
  • 🧱 Collateral: 20% of loan is withheld until full repayment
  • 🚨 Emergency Loans:
    • ⚡ Fast approval within 24 hours
    • 🧮 Max loan = 50% of total contributions
    • 💳 Flat 3% processing fee, repayable in 60 days at 7% interest

💼 Savings & Investment Opportunities

  • 💰 Regular Savings: Weekly contributions (e.g., KES 50) build financial discipline and loan eligibility.
  • 🌱 Group Investments: Opportunities in freelancing, e-commerce, agribusiness, and trading — funded through member capital.

🗳 Group Participation & Decision-Making

  • 📅 Monthly Meetings: On WhatsApp or in-person to review finances and plans
  • 🗳 Voting Rights: Every member helps shape group policy and financial decisions
